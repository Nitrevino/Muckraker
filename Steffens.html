Who was he?

Lincoln Steffens began his journalist career covering police corruption, urban politics, and reform efforts. His investigative work helped expose and defeat New York corrupt political candidates. He focused on political machinations within state capitals and investigated whether politicians represented their constituencies views.Later, he grew cynical over the complete unwilling for the “system” to reform, he eventually left muckraking and lived a semi-nomadic life for most of the rest of his life.
